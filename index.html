Biography

Simhala Mohana is a dedicated public servant with over 15 years of experience in politics. Born and raised in Colombo, he has been instrumental in bringing positive change to the community through education reforms and infrastructure development. His vision for a prosperous Sri Lanka has earned him widespread support across party lines.
